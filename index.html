About Me

Hi, I’m Shantanu Yadav, a forward-thinking software engineer based in India. I have a BCA degree and a deep interest in web development, cybersecurity, and data engineering. My journey in tech started with coding blogs and cybersecurity experiments back in school, where I developed a strong foundation in programming and technical troubleshooting.

Over the years, I've worked on multiple projects, from e-commerce solutions connecting local businesses to self-built applications for optimizing workflows. My current projects focus on leveraging technology to build intelligent and efficient solutions for the modern world, including RetailEdge, a self-checkout solution for retail and supermarket stores.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me exploring the latest advancements in AI, participating in tech conferences, or mentoring young developers. My goal is to make a meaningful impact by combining technical expertise with real-world applications.
